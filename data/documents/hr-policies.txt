Employees are expected to follow all company policies during their employment.
The organization promotes fairness, transparency, and equal opportunity.
All policies apply equally to full-time, part-time, and contract employees.
Failure to comply with policies may result in disciplinary action.
Employees should review policies regularly for updates.

Working hours are defined to ensure productivity and work-life balance.
Employees are required to log in during core business hours.
Flexible schedules may be approved by managers when required.
Overtime work must be approved in advance.
Attendance is monitored through the internal system.

Remote work is allowed based on role and business needs.
Employees must maintain reliable internet connectivity while working remotely.
Confidential information must be protected outside the office.
Managers may revoke remote access if performance issues arise.
Remote work policies may change based on company requirements.

Leave policies are designed to support employee well-being.
Employees are entitled to paid time off each year.
Leave requests should be submitted through the HR portal.
Unapproved absences may lead to warnings.
Unused leave may expire at the end of the year.

Sick leave is provided for health-related issues.
Medical certificates may be required for extended sick leave.
Employees should inform managers as early as possible.
Misuse of sick leave is strictly prohibited.
Health and safety are company priorities.

The company maintains a strict code of conduct.
Professional behavior is expected at all times.
Harassment or discrimination will not be tolerated.
Employees should report misconduct immediately.
Violations may lead to termination.

Confidentiality of company data is mandatory.
Employees must not share sensitive information externally.
Passwords and system access should not be shared.
Data breaches must be reported immediately.
Security training is provided periodically.

Performance reviews help measure employee growth.
Reviews are conducted at regular intervals.
Feedback is shared openly with employees.
Performance impacts promotions and bonuses.
Employees are encouraged to set improvement goals.

Training programs support skill development.
Employees may request approval for external courses.
Learning costs may be reimbursed if approved.
Training improves career progression.
Continuous learning is encouraged.

Employees must follow the exit process when resigning.
Notice periods must be served as per policy.
Company assets must be returned before exit.
Final settlements are processed after clearance.
Exit interviews help improve policies.
